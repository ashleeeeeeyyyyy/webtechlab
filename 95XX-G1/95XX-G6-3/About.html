What is Peace & Order?

Peace & Order is the sense of tranquility and unity under one community. In a community an individual must be living in peace and must express theirselves comfortably, to be able to live in peace even despite when it's night time. Peace & Order is where you would feel safe in a community and where everything is organized and just.

Everyone in the community especially the barangay officials works hard everyday in-order to make sure that the community is organized and checks whether everything is in place and that there are no destruction of peace within the barangay itself.
