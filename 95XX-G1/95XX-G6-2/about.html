About

Inclusion is the state of being counted in a community or a group of people.No matter what differences the people have,the people will always include everyone to the society.Inclusion is used to promote the rights of the the people with disabilities,In this way they can still express themselves to the society freely and without limitations.The main goal of Inclusion is to remove discrimination between people and other barriers such as inequality.This Baranggay the believe that Inclusion is growing day by day through by not talking about the differences that we have.

Person with disabilities will always have a day-to-day difficulties in the society.

That is why we treat we always treat them with consideration and also not to discriminate them

Inclusion is seen in our baranggay everyday.For Example,The Children plays with them unconditionally andIt is seen that kids are playing with one another after classes.Also in our public transportation,They will always have the privelege to go first of the the queue.The jeepney always saves a space for the PWDs and also for the senior citizens.

Education

So far in our baranggay,the kids with disabilities still go on the same school as the more fortunate ones.But they are treated cautiously to be more comfortable.

Lower Dagsian provides education that helps the children to perform well and promote good conduct.Despite not having a School that provides special education,The Nursery School has not yet encountered issues that has harmed the special ones.
